The Axis's Rise and Downfall

The Beginning

Germany and the Soviets quickly advanced into Poland. Poland quickly surrendered only months afterwards. German occupation of Poland was harsh, even questionable
today as being humane. The Soviets were almost the same. It even seemed to the Western World that they were allies. Though the Soviets did not think the same way. The
Sovies began secretely arming its nation for the oncoming war they knew would eventually happen. They used the blitzkrieg tactic to plow quickly into Poland taking most
of Poland even though the Soviets declared war on them first.

The Phony War and German Dominance

A period of 4-5 months known as the phony war occurred where little to no military activity occurred. This was merely Germany getting ready for an invasion into France.
All of a sudden, using blitzkrieg, Germany plowed through Belgium into France quickly making them surrender within months! Britain was forced back onto their island and to
defend for their lives on their own. Soon enough, Germany attacks the Soviets, just as they had expected but did not expect them to attack so soon. They took over most of
urban Russia in just a few months. It seemed to be the end of the Allies, but then the United States had joined the war because Japan attacked them. There was a glimmer of hope.

German Losses

Germany began losing in the Soviet Union when the Winter came. Especially at Stalingrad where over 500,000 Germans had died. The Germans made even more losses when D-day and
the Allied invasion of Italy happened which stretched out their resources. Soon enough Germany was being pushed on two fronts, with no where to go.

The End of Germany

Germany had lost too much. They had gone through 6 years of war and they were being starved of resources. Soon enough Germany has fallen to the Soviets an the Allies. Freedom
had won. Japan in the east had remained in the war. An invasion of their mainland was too costly, as proved by invasions of just small islands, where the Japanese would fight to
the last man! But the atom bomb has been built. They used it instead. On Hiroshima the Little Boy and on Nagasaki, the Fat Man. Japan had soon surrendered as well. The war was won.
The peoples of Europe had been freed. But at what cost? The Allies and the Soviets agreed this should never be allowed to happen again.
