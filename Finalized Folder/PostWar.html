Post War

At the end of the war at the year 1945,

The Nuremberg Trials begin to convict high ranking Nazi officials of war crimes including infamous officers and people who knew .

Hitler such as Joseph Goebbels, Hermann Goering and Josef Mengele

At the year 1946

Poland agreed to trade its eastern lands that it lost from the Soviets for parts of Eastern Germany

East Germany does not recognize this but nobody sees them as a threat.

At The year 1947,

A treaty is signed to end officially the war in Europe and Asia but this does not fit the economic downturn that is in Europe. This downturn can be described

as on the same levels of the German downturn in Pre-war. This is combated by the created of the EEC, the predecessor to the European Union. It is also helped by

the Marshall Plan from the United States.

Finally on the year 1948,

Berlin, which belongs to Western not Eastern Germany is cut off from supply. The Berlin Airlift begins and 1 plane flies over Berlin every so often

to drop off supplies. This begins the aggression between Soviet and American nations and begins the Cold War.
